Privacy Policy

At Tyro Ledger, we respect your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data.

No Data Collection

Tyro Ledger does not collect, store, or share any personal information. All data entered in the app is stored locally on your device.

Data Storage

All financial information and other app data remain on your device. We do not access or transmit this data to any servers or third parties.

Anonymous Usage Data

We may collect anonymous, aggregate data (such as app usage statistics) solely for the purpose of improving the app. This data does not identify you personally and is not shared with third parties.

Deleting Your Data

If you uninstall the app, all associated data will be permanently deleted from your device.
